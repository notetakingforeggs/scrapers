Station Checks

This website provides a live map of how recently nextbike stations in glasgow have been checked by distro/mobile mechanics.
To update the map, click the "update" button. You will need to link a Whatsapp account that is a member of the "station checks" Nextbike Whatsapp group.
Messages with station number in are processed and are inserted into a database before being displayed on the map.
